About

Louis Guichard is a photographer working with film and light. His images move between geometry and chance — the architecture of a street, the grain of a shadow, the quiet of a landscape met on the road.

Across series such as Light, Black & White, Life, On the Road and Hawaii, the work returns to a single question: how the everyday becomes something worth keeping.
